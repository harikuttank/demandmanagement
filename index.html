Role Accountability & Governance

Demand Manager / PMO

Owns process execution and audit trails. Runs G1 Triage, orchestrates shaping, coordinates scoring, maintains system of record.

Portfolio Council (PRB)

Governance body for G2 Prioritization. Reviews scores, applies capacity constraints, makes trade-off decisions.

Investment Board (IRB)

Governance body for G3 Funding. Approves refined business cases and authorizes funding reallocation.
